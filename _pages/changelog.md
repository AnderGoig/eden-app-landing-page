## Eden for iOS

Eden was created for Envato Market authors who want a focused way to follow store performance on iPhone.

#### Included

- Earnings summaries with day, month, and year filters.
- Sales and statement history.
- Portfolio item performance overview.
- Push notifications for new sales.
- Multi-account support.
- Offline access for previously loaded data.
